# ALBA Wellbeing

## Design Context

### Users
People seeking calm, trusted human support — companionship, wellbeing, hypnotherapy, Reiki,
life transitions, elderly care, end-of-life and bereavement. Often vulnerable or in transition
(illness, recovery, ageing, grief, loneliness), or their family members researching on their
behalf. Read on phones and desktops, often quietly and unhurried. Served by Lina Simavičiūtė
(Alba) in Louth, Lincolnshire, surrounding areas, and online.

### Brand Personality
Warm · human · trustworthy. Calm-luxury, never clinical-cold. "Professional Knowledge. Human
Presence. A Happy We." The interface should feel safe, respectful, unhurried, and quietly
premium — like an editorial wellbeing magazine, not a medical portal.

### Aesthetic Direction
Calm-luxury wellness, light theme (anxious/late-night readers want light, not glowing dark).
Cream / warm beige surfaces, chocolate/cocoa text, soft bronze accent. Hand-drawn "Alba figure"
emblem + coffee-ground "Soul Print" footprint (favicon/brand mark). Editorial spacing, soft
rounded sections, subtle warm shadows, gentle scroll motion. No bright colors, no AI-slop
gradients, no clinical sterility. Anti-references: generic SaaS template, cold medical clinic,
neon/dark dashboard.

### Design Principles
1. **Warm, never sterile** — every surface tinted toward the brand's warm hue; no pure black/white/gray.
2. **Unhurried hierarchy** — generous, varied spacing; one clear message per section.
3. **Human over corporate** — first-person voice, real artwork, soft edges, gentle motion.
4. **Accessible calm** — WCAG AA contrast, visible focus, reduced-motion respected; readers may be tired or anxious.
5. **Quiet luxury** — restraint and craft over decoration; the bronze accent is rare and therefore meaningful.

### Stack / conventions
Static HTML + CSS + vanilla JS, no build. Type: Cormorant Garamond (display) + Manrope (body).
Tokens in `:root` (cream/cocoa/bronze, `--shadow-*`, `--radius-*`). Quality bar: **flagship**
(ready for a real premium business).
