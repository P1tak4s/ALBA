About Lina

A lifelong calling to care

I come from a family with three generations dedicated to health and care. I trained as a doctor in Lithuania, and later added studies in public health, hypnotherapy, Reiki and holistic wellbeing.

Over the years I have supported people through illness, recovery, loneliness, grief, ageing and end-of-life. Since 2022 I have been doing this work here in the UK.

I believe wellbeing and happiness aren’t accidents. They grow through trust, understanding, acceptance, gratitude, meaningful connection and conscious choice.

A combination of professional knowledge and human presence.
