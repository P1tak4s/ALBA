What ALBA stands for

Helping without taking away independence

Direct, personal care built on trust — a relationship formed directly with the person and their family, with consistency, presence and mutual respect.

  • Respect for the person, family, culture, language, traditions, beliefs, values and choices.
  • Listening before acting. Supporting independence rather than replacing it.
  • Professional knowledge combined with genuine human presence.
  • Conditions for comfort, dignity, wellbeing and the best possible quality of life.
