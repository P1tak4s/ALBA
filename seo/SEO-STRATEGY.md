## 2. The core strategic insight

ALBA competes in **two different search markets** that need different tactics:

1. **Local companionship / care (Louth & Lincolnshire)** — the SERPs are owned by national agencies with high domain authority (Promedica24, Helping Hands, Elder, Blossom Home Care, The Great Care Company) and aggregators (Lottie, Age UK). **You will not out-rank them on "home care Louth" head terms** for 12+ months. Instead, win the *underserved, differentiated* long-tail they ignore: *private companion*, *independent companionship*, *wellbeing companion*, *end-of-life companion*, *appointment companion*, *retired doctor companion*.

2. **Hypnotherapy / Reiki / wellbeing (local + online)** — competitors are *individual practitioners* of comparable authority (Kintsugi, The Gentle Mind, Mandy Fuller, Christina Myers). This is **winnable** with on-page SEO, professional-register listings (GHR), and ALBA's unique doctor credential. The **online** hypnotherapy market also lets you rank UK-wide, not just locally.

**Plus a near-empty niche:** *private end-of-life / palliative companionship* and *bereavement companionship* as a paid independent service has very thin organic competition in Lincolnshire. High differentiation, high emotional intent, low supply. Make it a flagship pillar.

## 3. The wedge: E-E-A-T as the moat

Lina's credentials are the single biggest SEO asset and almost no local competitor can match them. Every page must surface them:

- **Experience** — caregiving since 2022 in the UK; lived family background of three generations in care.
- **Expertise** — GP qualification, MSc Public Health, hypnotherapy/Reiki/homeopathy training.
- **Authoritativeness** — professional-register memberships (see action: join **GHR / CNHC / a Reiki federation**), directory listings, reviews.
- **Trust** — transparent pricing, Client Agreement & Confidentiality page, "not a replacement for medical care" honesty, free consultation.

> A dedicated, schema-marked **About/Lina** page with credentials, photo, and `Person` markup is the highest-leverage single page on the site.

## 5. Strategic pillars (priority order)

1. **Custom domain + Google Business Profile** — non-negotiable foundation. `alba-navy.vercel.app` cannot build trust or rank well. Register a branded `.co.uk`, then create & verify a GBP **Service-Area** listing.
2. **Local SEO & citations** — consistent NAP everywhere; list on Hypnotherapy Directory, GHR, CNHC, Bark, Yell, Lottie/Age UK partner lists, Apple/Bing Maps.
3. **Convert the one-pager into a proper architecture** — distinct, indexable Service pages, an E-E-A-T About page, a Pricing page, and a small set of location/service-area pages (see `SITE-STRUCTURE.md`).
4. **Reviews engine** — actively collect Google reviews after every engagement; reviews are a top-3 local ranking and conversion factor.
5. **Helpful, experience-led content** — a modest blog answering the real questions families ask (see `CONTENT-CALENDAR.md`), reinforcing E-E-A-T and capturing informational long-tail.
6. **Technical & schema foundation** — `LocalBusiness`/`HealthAndBeautyBusiness`, `Person`, `Service`, `FAQPage` JSON-LD; sitemap, robots, fast Core Web Vitals (already strong — static site).

## 6. Constraints & risks

- **Solo operator, limited time/budget.** This plan is *right-sized*: a handful of excellent pages + steady reviews beats dozens of thin pages. Do **not** mass-produce location pages (quality-gate: warn at 30, hard stop at 50 — we stay under ~8).
- **YMYL territory.** Health/end-of-life content is "Your Money or Your Life" — Google holds it to high E-E-A-T standards. Keep the medical-disclaimer honesty, cite credentials, never over-claim outcomes.
- **Regulated-care confusion.** Be explicit that ALBA offers *companionship & wellbeing*, not regulated personal/nursing care (CQC) — this is both a legal clarity and an SEO positioning advantage.
- **Capacity ceiling.** One person can only serve so many clients; over-optimising for high-volume head terms wastes effort. Target high-intent, high-margin long-tail.
