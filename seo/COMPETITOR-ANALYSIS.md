# ALBA Wellbeing — Competitor Analysis

_Based on live SERP research for Louth / Lincolnshire, June 2026._

## Two competitive sets

ALBA's services straddle two SERPs with very different competitive dynamics.

---

## Set A — Companionship & home care (Louth/Lincolnshire)

These rank for "home care / companionship / live-in care Louth". Mostly **national agencies with high domain authority** and regulated (CQC) personal-care offerings — a different product to ALBA's independent companionship, but they intercept the same searchers.

| Competitor | Type | Strength | Gap ALBA can exploit |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Promedica24](https://promedica24homecare.co.uk/home-care-louth/) | National live-in agency (~£1,295/wk) | High DA, location pages, CQC | Expensive, impersonal, agency-rotated carers; no wellbeing/therapy |
| [Helping Hands](https://www.helpinghandshomecare.co.uk/our-locations/louth/) | National visiting + live-in | Huge DA, brand, 30-min visits | Commodity care; no doctor, no end-of-life *companionship* angle |
| [Elder](https://www.elder.org/our-locations/east-midlands/lincolnshire/louth/) | Live-in marketplace | SEO-heavy location pages | Marketplace, not a person; no local human face |
| [Blossom Home Care](https://www.blossomhomecare.co.uk/lincolnshire/louth/) | Regional franchise | Local-ish, 24h support | Franchise; no therapeutic/wellbeing depth |
| [The Great Care Company](https://thegreatcarecompany.com/locations/louth/) | Lincolnshire care co. | Local relevance | Standard domiciliary care; no hypnotherapy/Reiki |
| [Lottie](https://lottie.org/...louth/) / [Age UK Lincs](https://www.cancersupportlincolnshire.nhs.uk/...) | Aggregators / charity | Very high DA, directory | **List ALBA on these** rather than fight them |

**Takeaway:** Don't compete head-on for "home care Louth". Own the **independent / private / premium companionship** long-tail these agencies don't target, and the **wellbeing + end-of-life companionship** angle they don't offer. Get *listed* on the aggregators (Lottie, Age UK partner directories) for referral + citation value.

### Differentiated terms to own (low agency competition)
`private companion Louth` · `independent companionship Lincolnshire` · `wellbeing companion` · `companion for hospital appointments Lincolnshire` · `private end of life companion` · `palliative companion at home Lincolnshire` · `overnight companion / sitting service Louth` · `retired GP companion care`

## Keyword-gap summary

- **Uncontested / thin:** private end-of-life companion, palliative companionship at home, bereavement companion, doctor-led wellbeing companion, appointment accompaniment Lincolnshire.
- **Winnable with effort:** hypnotherapy Louth (+ long-tails), Reiki Louth, online hypnotherapy UK, wellbeing support Lincolnshire, life transition support.
- **Don't fight (yet):** home care Louth, live-in care Louth, care agency Louth — owned by high-DA national agencies; pursue only via aggregator listings.

## Three biggest opportunities

1. **Be the only doctor-led wellbeing companion in Lincolnshire** — bake "doctor / GP" into titles, H1s, About, and schema.
2. **Own the end-of-life / palliative companionship niche** — emotionally urgent, high-value, almost no organic competition.
3. **Win online hypnotherapy long-tail** — escapes the local ceiling entirely; one strong page can rank UK-wide.
