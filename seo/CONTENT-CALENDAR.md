# ALBA Wellbeing — Content Calendar

**Cadence:** realistic for a solo practitioner = **2 posts / month** (every other week). Quality and lived experience beat volume — each post should answer a real question a client or family member has actually asked, in Lina's voice.

**Voice rules:** first person, warm, honest; lead with experience; always include the medical-disclaimer where relevant; link to 2–3 service pages; end with the free-consultation CTA. These are YMYL topics — accuracy and credentials matter.

## Content pillars

1. **Companionship & ageing** (family decision-makers searching for help)
2. **End-of-life & grief** (high emotional intent, low competition — flagship)
3. **Hypnotherapy & the mind** (online reach, winnable terms)
4. **Wellbeing & "transparent happiness"** (brand philosophy, top-of-funnel)
5. **Local / Lincolnshire** (local relevance + internal links to area pages)

## Non-blog content (compounds SEO)
- **Google Business Profile posts** — 1–2/month (offers, tips, photos); GBP activity helps local ranking.
- **FAQ expansion** — every real question a client asks → add to the relevant service page's FAQ (feeds `FAQPage` schema + AI answers).
- **Reviews** — request after every engagement; reply to each (reviews are content too).
- **Original photography** — Lina, calm session settings, Louth/Lincolnshire context (no stock).

## Production guardrails
- 2 posts/month is the floor *and* ceiling — don't sacrifice quality for volume.
- Never publish thin or AI-generic YMYL content; Google penalises it hardest in health.
- Repurpose: each post → 1 GBP post + 1 social caption.
