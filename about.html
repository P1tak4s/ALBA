About

Hello, my name is Lina — known to many as Alba.

Doctor. Caregiver. Therapist. Artist. And, most of all, a steady, caring presence for the people I support.

Book a free consultation See how I can help
Lina Simavičiūtė · Alba

My interest in health, wellbeing and human experience has been a lifelong journey.

I was born in Lithuania in 1975, into a family with three generations dedicated to health and care. It felt natural to follow that path.

I trained as a doctor at Kaunas University of Medicine and qualified as a General Practitioner. Along the way I also gained Master’s degrees in Public Health, in International Relations and Diplomacy, and in Law and Administration.

Alongside medicine, I studied hypnotherapy, Reiki, massage, homeopathy, coaching and holistic wellbeing — because caring for people is about so much more than treating symptoms.

For many years I have walked beside people through illness, recovery, disability, loneliness, grief, ageing and end-of-life. Since 2022, I have been doing this work here in the UK.

The foundation of my work is a combination of professional knowledge and human presence.

What I believe

Happiness is not perfection

It can live alongside challenge, grief, uncertainty and change. It begins when we accept ourselves honestly, with openness and compassion.

I believe wellbeing and happiness aren’t accidents. They grow through awareness, trust, understanding, acceptance, gratitude, meaningful relationships and conscious choice.

My role is not to tell people who they should be. It is to help create the conditions where they can understand themselves a little better, see their options clearly, and make their own right choices — moving forward with greater comfort, meaning and quality of life.
